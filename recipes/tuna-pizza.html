Recipe For Tuna Pizza

Description

Only a few common paultry ingredients are needed in making this homemade tuna pizza.

You can make pizza dough as well as pizza sauce from scratch following the recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pizza dough (external link)
  • 3 tablespoons of pizza sauce (external link)
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese
  • 5 ounces (150 grams) canned tuna, drained of oil
  • 1/2 cup of sweetcorn
  • basil leaves

Steps

  1. Roll out the pizza dough. If you like thin crust, roll as thin as possible.
  2. When ready, transfer the rolled-out dough onto a baking sheet lined with baking parchment paper.
  3. Top it with half of the sauce, shredded cheese, tuna and sweetcorn.
  4. Bake in the oven at 400° Fahrenheit (200° Celsius) for 10 minutes or until the bottom is nice and brown.
  5. Enjoy!
